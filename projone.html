Steering Wheel Design


In 2018, my job as an ergonomics engineer on Northwestern's Baja racing team was to design a comfortable steering wheel.

The steering wheel was modeled in Solidworks.

I ensured the wheel was ergonomic by 3D printing sections and asking team members to hold the wheel.

The final wheel was made of waterjet aluminum, with a 3D printed ABS exterior.
